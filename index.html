Projects

Data Center Power Consumption Analysis

Sep 2025 - Nov 2026

Problem: Energy costs account for a significant share of data center operating expenses. We analyzed electricity metrics from an Ecuador-based data center to forecast energy demand and detect anomalous consumption patterns.

Data: Ecuador data center power metrics dataset containing active / reactive energy, power factor, and environmental indicators.

Approach: Built regression models (Linear Regression, Random Forest Regression, SVR) on the training set to predict Active Energy; trained classification models (XGBoost, Logistic Regression, Random Forest) to categorize server load types. Applied grid / random search for hyperparameter tuning with 10-fold cross-validation and early stopping to prevent overfitting.

Outcome / Impact: Regression models quantified future energy demand to support capacity planning and adaptive cooling; classification models identified abnormal energy usage (e.g., hardware faults) for timely intervention. Compared multi-model results on the test set to select the best-performing pipeline.

My Contribution: Independently developed all regression and classification pipelines, conducted hyperparameter tuning, and performed comparative analysis across models on the test set.

McDonald's Review Sentiment Classification

2025 - 2026 (Course Project)

Problem: Classifying McDonald's customer reviews into positive, neutral, and negative sentiments to help the brand identify pain points and improve service quality.

Data: McDonald's review dataset with text reviews and star ratings; preprocessed into train / test splits.

Approach: Explored multiple text embedding strategies - Word2Vec, GloVe, and BERT - combined with classical ML models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost) and PySpark-based pipelines (Decision Tree, GBT via OneVsRest). Applied TF-IDF, n-gram features, hyperparameter tuning via grid search, and cross-validation.

Outcome / Impact: Achieved competitive accuracy on 3-class sentiment classification; identified data leakage risks and proposed mitigation strategies for future iterations.

My Contribution: Built the full embedding pipeline (Word2Vec, GloVe, BERT), trained and evaluated multiple classifiers, and documented findings on feature importance and data quality issues.
